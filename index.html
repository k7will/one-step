South Korean politician serving as a member of the National Assembly and the leader of the Democratic Party of Korea. Lee was the nominee of the Democratic Party in the 2022 South Korean presidential election. He was the 35th Governor of Gyeonggi Province from 2018 to 2021.

Born to a poor family in Andong, Lee became a factory worker after primary school and became disabled due to workplace injuries.
Lee earned middle and high school equivalency diplomas and studied at Chung-Ang University, earning his law degree in 1986.
As a human rights and labour lawyer, Lee organized with Minbyun and advocated for opening a new hospital in Seongnam.
